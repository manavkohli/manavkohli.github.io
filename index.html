Intro

I grew up in Portland, OR and am currently studying Computer Science at Claremont McKenna College. I love to build things, and CS seemed like the most obvious way to learn, challenge myself, and practically apply something that can be abstract.

I have a lot of ideas, and am willing to bet 5% of them are good. Some of the better include a basic community music listening web app I built, some research I pursued on binary star mass transfer, and 'Refridge-raiders', a refridgerator cleaning service.

I took my first flight alone when I was six years old and have been fortunate enough to keep traveling since. In the last three years I visited 4 continents and 9 countries, meanwhile working on a farm exchange in Brazil, traveling and interning in Mongolia, and backpacking through central Europe.
